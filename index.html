Campus Life

school church

was built in 1978

Stevens Creek Church is a a regular church near Stevens Creek but what makes this different is this is also where people graduating from 5th to 6th grade will go to on graduation night.



school library

was built in 1980

Stevens Creek Library is an absolute neccesity, we provide great literature for every genre, novel and book that's been here. This is also why our school gets a higher than average reading proficiency.

school auditorium

was built in 1982

Most schools wouldn't have a school auditorium but thanks to the money the PTO(Parent Teacher Org) has raised for the school. The school was able to purchase a auditorium for assemblies, music madness and other events.
